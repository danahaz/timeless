Purchase & Payment Policy

At Timeless Jewelry, we aim to make your shopping experience seamless and secure. Below are the terms and guidelines for purchasing and payment on our website.

Accepted Payment Methods

  • We accept the following payment methods:
    • Credit and Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express).
    • Bank Transfers (available for select regions).
    • Digital Wallets.
  • All payments are processed securely to ensure the safety of your personal information.

Order Confirmation

  • Once your payment is successfully processed, you will receive an order confirmation email with your order details.
  • If you do not receive a confirmation email, please check your spam/junk folder or contact us at customer.service@timelessjewelry.com.

Pricing and Currency

  • All prices are listed in US dollar. For international customers, the total amount will be converted to your local currency by your bank or payment provider.
  • Taxes, duties, and additional fees (if applicable) will be calculated at checkout and included in your total.

Installment Payments

  • We offer flexible installment payment options for eligible orders. Please check the "Installments" option during checkout for more details.
  • Failure to complete installment payments as agreed may result in cancellation or additional fees.

Failed or Declined Payments

  • If your payment is declined, please verify your payment details and try again.
  • For assistance, contact your bank or our customer service team at customer.service@timelessjewelry.com.

Secure Transactions

  • We use industry-standard encryption to protect your payment information during transactions.
  • Your privacy and security are our priority. Please review our Privacy Policy for more details.
